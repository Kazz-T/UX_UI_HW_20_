YAPS

Not for profit website redesign challenge

THE BRIEF: Choose a nonprofit and execute the user experience (UX) / user interface (UI) design process.
THE SOLUTION: Redesign YAPS using high-fidelity responsive web design (RWD) solution through a clickable UI prototype (Figma, Adobe XD or InVision).
MY ROLE: UX testing, Information Architecture, UI design and prototyping.
TOOLS: MIRO, Figma, Figjam
DURATION: 3 weeks

THE RESEARCH

Taking what we have learnt so far from our own analysis and user research, the team devised our problem statement…

“Ethically conscious people that are looking to adopt a pet for the first time are feeling overwhelmed when researching into selecting the right pet. How might we provide easily accessible information and build trust so that they can make an informed decision on whether they adopt a pet and what pet to choose.”

… ideated and prioritised features...

... and created a user journey map.

Through our research a key requirement was to look at redesigning the existing IA to improve user experience as well as promote boarding services and donations for the business. The team undertook a card sorting activity to inform the redesign.

SOLUTION DESIGN AND TESTING

Along with the team, I created selected pages for our designs. Our low fi prototype was used to conduct usability tests covering the following tasks.

TASK 1: Adopting a pet

TASK 2: Making a donation

TASK 3: Pet Boarding

TASK 4: Business Sponsor

Testing outcomes saw the team make 3 key changes to the design and was iterated into the final hi-fi design and prototype.

Iteration 1

Users were confused on the wording of Boarding Pets. Based on feedback, we updated this to Cattery & Kennels.

Iteration 2

When users were asked to make a donation, they did not select How You Can Help where this page was located. The label was updated to Help Us to help users scan the menu quickly and navigate to the correct page.

Iteration 3

All users navigated to Contact Us, when asked to find out how to become a business sponsor. This section was placed into the Contact Us page.
